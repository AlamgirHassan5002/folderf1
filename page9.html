Aurus

Aurus is a Russian luxury car manufacturer brand. Aurus was founded in 2018. The Aurus Senat is part of the Kortezh series, which will include a sedan, a minivan and an off-road vehicle. The Senat was shown publicly for the first time at the Moscow International Automobile Salon in September 2018. Currently, Aurus Senat limousine is used by President and Prime Minister of Russian Federation.
