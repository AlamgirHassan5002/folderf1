BMW

Bayerische Motoren Werke AG, commonly referred to as BMW, is a German multinational company which produces luxury vehicles and motorcycles. The company was founded in 1916 as a manufacturer of aircraft engines, which it produced from 1917 until 1918 and again from 1933 to 1945. In 2015, BMW was the world's twelfth-largest producer of motor vehicles. The company has significant motorsport history, especially in touring cars, Formula 1, sports cars and the Isle of Man TT.
